# Rubric changelog

All changes to the measurement standard are recorded here. Version bump rules are in `docs/versioning.md`.

## 1.0.0

Initial rubric. Three axes:

- Greenfield vs Brownfield
- Interrogative vs Opinionated
- Autonomous vs Human-in-loop

Sign conventions, weights, and answer-to-value mappings are a first proposal and are expected to change before this rubric is treated as stable. Any change that can move a score for identical evidence will bump the MAJOR version.
